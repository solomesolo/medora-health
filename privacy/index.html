Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Medora ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information we collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, for example when you use our contact form or email us. We may also automatically collect certain technical information about your device and how you use the site, including through cookies, as described in our Cookie settings.

3. How we use your information

We use this information to respond to inquiries, operate and improve the website, analyse usage in line with your cookie choices, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies where necessary for the site to work, and optional analytics cookies if you allow them. You can change your choices at any time on our Cookie settings page.

5. Legal bases (GDPR)

Where the GDPR applies, we process personal data on the basis of contract (e.g. responding to your request), legitimate interests (operating the site, security), or consent (non-essential cookies), as appropriate.

6. Retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for these purposes, unless a longer period is required by law.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data, restrict or object to processing, data portability, withdraw consent, and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

8. Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data. No transmission over the internet is completely secure.
